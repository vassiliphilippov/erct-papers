abstract: >-
  This study assessed the effects of incorporating 3 different
  interventions into parts of physical education classes on anxiety
  symptoms in high school students. A parallel, 4-arm randomized
  clinical trial was conducted with technical high school students
  from 2 campuses in Brazil. The interventions lasted 12 weeks and
  included diaphragmatic breathing exercises, aerobic and resistance
  exercises, and cooperative sports activities, alongside a control
  group that followed the standard curriculum. Anxiety symptoms were
  assessed with the Generalized Anxiety Disorder-7 scale before and
  after the intervention.
